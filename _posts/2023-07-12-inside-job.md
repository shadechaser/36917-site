The good news is after more than 5 decades of research I’ve discovered the one thing completely outside myself that is 100% responsible for how I deal with my mistakes, failures, and unfair suffering.

The bad news is I lied. The one thing is me. It’s an inside job. 

And the *interesting* thing is that if I spend enough time contemplating a myriad of spiritual paths, the differences between inside or outside fall away like an old snakeskin.
