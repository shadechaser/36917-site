Step back Mighty West!
Because before all your structured stricture;
before words' purpose were hijacked to gorge on images and ration their light;
before god was caught, bought, and taught ~
He was sought

Step back, earnest East!
Because that seeking was never profane;
it was always the inbreath of spirit's outbreath,
Always and Ever, one step closer than intimate

Step in, East and West!
Hear your mystics roar in stain glassed gardens;
ply the light that buys the light that knits the shadows
and pulls them like velvet from an event horizon

Where all that is left is 
me,  watching through god's eyes 
as *She* sees *me*

And I know without question
I am Love
