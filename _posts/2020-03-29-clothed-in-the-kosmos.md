---
title: "Clothed in the Kosmos"
date: 2020-03-29
layout: post
category: prose
style: verse
---

I'm wearing clothes
Made of of the fabric of the Kosmos
Love currents and conundrums the stars and moons; 
It rivers and snakes around the jacket and the pants
Irridescence running errands from the absolute
Pastels and erases itself in This,
Love's laughter; silence.
