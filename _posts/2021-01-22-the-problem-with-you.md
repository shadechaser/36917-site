---
title: "the problem with you"
date: 2021-01-22
layout: post
category: prose
style: verse
---

the problem with you is that
I need to go deep
the weather is tired;
foodstuff and fashion are spooning

it's the flickering behind the lids
that sirens me

tension brines the space between
disappears
into the dark pools
or dangerous wonder
or other
all depending on you

the problem with you is that
you are the world
and without boundaries
I am you.
