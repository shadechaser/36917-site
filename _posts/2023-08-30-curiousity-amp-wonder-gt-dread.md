---
title: "Curiousity &amp; Wonder &gt; Dread"
date: 2023-08-30
layout: post
category: prose
style: verse
---

Friend just sent me a before and after picture of fires around their place in Sushwap.  I suggested lining the pictures up in her head - one of before,  one of after,  and another one for "next".  Not future,  just "next".  What will it look like.  Worse? Better?  Both?   

Felt great seeing dread replaced with a sense of curiousity and wonder.
