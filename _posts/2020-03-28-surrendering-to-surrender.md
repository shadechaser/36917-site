---
title: "Surrendering to Surrender"
date: 2020-03-28
layout: post
category: prose
style: verse
---

I told you yesterday I would sit on it, and I did. Here's the simplest way I can put it:  The way out is Through,  and the path Through is Surrender.

Stop at the start of the path,  and add all your expectations to the funeral pyre that was lit by those before you. Stay long enough to be warmed by the flames consuming all your 'Shoulds' and 'Musts'.  Know that you are being held in an inconceivably complete embrace by the  cosmos itself;    and if you are still stuck in brittle,  frozen fear,  close your eyes,  and ask...

"Please help me to surrender to Surrender."
