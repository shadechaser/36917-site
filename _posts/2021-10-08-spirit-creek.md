while i wouldn't recommend the path that  unfolded after you labelled me, i have to admit its embrace was remarkable

~

the mind is playing whack-a-mole with the exact turn of phrase;
elitist? zealot? moron? ideologue?   

it's ghosting me -  an independent heartbeat just below the preverbal;  bouncing between Left and Right,  never quite showing its face,  just footprinting intent as it steps over choice and bends the larynx to its will

~

let's both forgive the argument that is now unfolding;
simply watch as Spirt flays the rhetoric and fallacies from the substance
and drops us on either side of a creek
where we can dip our feet in,
make I contact 
and be a part of the Divide instead of being separated by it.
