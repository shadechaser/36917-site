---
title: "In a departed’s shadow: by yourself but not alone"
date: 2020-04-06
layout: post
category: prose
style: verse
---

When you picture a  soul that left too soon;  you're both bowing your heads in humility at the altar; music drapes you in  redemption; tears cloud and cleanse;  then you continue on your path by yourself,  but not quite alone.
