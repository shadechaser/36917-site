date: 2020-04-06

---

Listening to Glen Gould is almost embarrassingly intimate.  Naked, distilled genius that spoons the cerebrum. There is no separation between his notes and his life;  sad,  euphoric,  shining and shrouded.   Like someone made one large score with every note he ever played,  unfurled his soul over it and let it melt into the notes and fill the spaces between.
