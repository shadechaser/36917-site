title: "You’re exactly where you’re supposed to be"
date: 2020-04-05
layout: post
category: prose
style: verse
---

I'll admit when you told me I was always exactly where I should be I thought you were full of shit.  Until  I realized three things:

1) If I imagine a perfect state down the road,  with every one of my needs met,  that imagining always,  always happens *here, now*.

2)  If that imagined perfect state ever happens,  it will always happen *here, now*. 

3) I'm always ever only wanting a perfect state (Now - see point one);  experiencing it (Now - see point two),  or not caring either way (interestingly,  always Now as well)

This is It.  And It always has its arms impossibly wide open for you.
