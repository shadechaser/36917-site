---
title: "Acceptance, Willingness, Grace"
date: 2020-03-31
layout: post
category: prose
style: verse
---

Acceptance is landing face first in hell,  shitting judgement out like a bad dinner ,  and looking for a way out.

Willingness is searing your fingerprints off stacking oven-fresh bricks like a giant Jenga tower to get out

Grace is seeing that the person who pushed you in can now walk across the fresh bricks you laid down,  and avoid the trip themselves.
