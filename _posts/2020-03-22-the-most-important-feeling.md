To be honest, you caught me off guard when you asked me what the most important Feeling was. I Broca-bleached the question till it was just this side of lifeless, then sent it down an ancient ladder to the heart-lopedia, where this was returned:

“The problem is in the question. It’s not *what* is the most important Feeling, but *when*? The answer is always, always, always whatever is being felt Now.”
