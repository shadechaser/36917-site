date: 2022-12-19

perhaps anxiety and sleepiness
are not antipidodes to be integrated
but expressions of One Love
pushing at the surface like fingers in a balloon

north and south and east and west
all moving outward so you can explore the
sacred geography of You
in the safety of your own (h)aum

maybe the places that used to masquerade as 
edges between unbearable, asleep, and This
have disrobed for a reason ~
unveiling the Essence
that has grace-noted mystic's lives
for millenium

liminal space,  This,  
where god's supply line
braids cortisol and melatonin
into filaments that thread 
your nervous system
simultaneously pointing you away
and bringing you home

This is where you can find
impossibilities budding:

full emptiness
dialectics of One
suffering's grace

sub ek;

love
