the havoc you raise ~ 
by moving around instead of Through 
outside ~ hopping conversations 
inside ~ skipping o'er neurons; 
~the havoc is significant  

Spiritual steel wool,  this century's hair shirt; 
Ignore what you feel 
and you will feel what you ignore ~  
~the correlation is exponential  

The part of you that wailed in your forced detachment 
Cried in your first loss 
Sighed in darkened cross roads 
and atrophied o'er the years  

never 
went 
away  

Just made rabbit warrens in your soul's geography 
Where you would look for YOU unsuccessfully   

Until,  with your second to last teacher watching, 
with your penultimate effort to Become waning 
with Id scraping shadow children to wallow in the chaos  

you cried 
~ collapsed in on yourself ~  
became the YOU  
watching the you ~  

spiritual cross multiplication, cancelling redundant letters; until   

just YOU was left ~  
finally ready to move through.
