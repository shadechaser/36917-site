Speechless,  but not wordless.   

My ‘whys’ were replaced by ‘thank yous’ this morning,  birthing god’s haiku ~
A brook with 17 stepping stones that led directly to Eckart Tolle’s roadhouse.

Infinite rooms and hallways there,   as blessings erased boundaries in the Now:

All shall be well
May you be filled with loving kindness
Ya Hadi
L’Chaim

~ 

sub ek
