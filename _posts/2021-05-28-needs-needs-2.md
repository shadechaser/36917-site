---
title: "Need’s Needs"
date: 2021-05-28
layout: post
category: prose
style: verse
---

just after dawn i spoke to god and supplied the list that needed  to be fulfilled for my happiness, peace, and well being for the day 

 up till noon i repeatedly reminded God of the list, being careful to capitalize his name and repeat the ministrations for added gravity 

 in the afternoon, disappointment and doubt swept the prayer room clean.  i watched god clothe the rest of the day in weather, conflict, people and story perfectly fitted to the peaks and valleys of my need's needs.   

 i exhaled,  and asked to remember the lesson come the next dawn.
