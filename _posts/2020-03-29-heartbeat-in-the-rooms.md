---
title: "Heartbeat in the Rooms"
date: 2020-03-29
layout: post
category: prose
style: verse
---

We form a heartbeat in the Rooms that changes minute to minute, hour to hour, 24 by 24.

It's as unique, and scarred, and beautiful, as each of us filling a chair.

Willingness keeps the pulse alive; grace carries it from room to room, and compassion binds it in the body of Recovery.
