---
title: "Prayer for the City"
date: 2020-03-21
layout: post
category: prose
style: verse
---

God’s body is the landscape before me today
Buildings and road-ribboned spaces her skin
May I be one of the masses peopling her bright arteries
Opened by grace and currented by willingness
Doing for others what I cannot do for myself
Always knowing the outcome is beyond my control
