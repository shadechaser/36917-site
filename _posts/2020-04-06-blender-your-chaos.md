date: 2020-04-06

Stuck again?  Angry as hell?  Picture putting all your chaos into a blender.  Anger, sadness, emptiness, resentments, sickness, mania...everything.  Start it at 1, then get everyone  on your resento-meter to turn it up  higher and higher.  Finally,  take that sludge, will it to light,  and release into a moon draped forest.
