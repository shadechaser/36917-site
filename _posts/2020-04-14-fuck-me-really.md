"Fuck Me" Really? 

The following are always available to me,  if I look: 

A moment in the past week where I received less than I wanted but more than I deserved; 
Somebody in my circle who is suffering less than me right now;  and somebody who is suffering more,  
The chance to offer less opinions and more empathy 

Comparsion doesn't have to be just the death of joy,  as Mark Twain holds,  it can be its resurrection as well.
