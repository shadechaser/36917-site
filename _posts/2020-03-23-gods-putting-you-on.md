date: 2020-03-23

There are an exact number of spaces that will open to you today,  and allow you 
To just Be.

This is when god can step in,  and put you on like a silk shirt
When She is clothed in you, hidden miracles are birthed
When He is clothed in us, miracles are knitted together like a quilt

This is when ~ 
the lonely are heard
the hungry are fed

the angry are salved 
~and the scared are embraced

There are an exact number of spaces that will open to you tomorrow
To just be

This is when god can step in,  and put you on like a shirt….
