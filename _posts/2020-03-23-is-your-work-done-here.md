Questions to see if your work is done here.

Can you feel without thinking about feeling? If the answer is no,  there’s work to do.

Can you do without thinking about doing?  If the answer is no,  there’s work to do.

Can you help at least one other person with 1  or 2?  If the answer is yes,  there’s work do do.
