Evergreen-ed rain is the shoo-in favourite, but there **are** proxies:

Heat-laden aspen whisper breezes;
Northern flickers' call and response,

Or afternoons like today when the canopy wears Unusual like a pheromone,
silently nudging me past **that** trail head and **that** one and **that** one to 
an xy coordinate that never shows up on the GPS's after-scrawl

~
These are more holy Whens than Wheres;
expressions of geography no more constrained by cause and effect
than Grace or coincidence

Warrens of Now,
where volition and fate have both taken early retirement
and the only prescriptive left is to move from human being
to Human,  Being

~

sub ek
