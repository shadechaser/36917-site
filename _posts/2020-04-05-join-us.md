Join Us

It's easier just after the rain starts
to knit the impossibilities of the day into a wearable;
dance between drops to the edge of the moment,
and salt the silence with notes that will ladder you to us

Step by step, scale up an octave minus one
into the persistent dawn channeled with sweetwater

Sing with us;  glory in the delta's delta

Backlight  the answer,
Breech the banks

Become the rain that falls on the impossibilities of another's day
