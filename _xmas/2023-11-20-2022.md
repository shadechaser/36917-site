Tried some spiritual origami this year;  luckily there was no shortage of paper.

You can't start folding without knowing your Source,  of course,  
so I took my time looking over the plane before it was sacrificed
to the creases and folds of Becoming:

Silken papyrus fibres, spun from the grace and chaos of the pandemic's first two years
and woven by THIS year into a sheet of strength and subtlety.

Years begat Source,  Source begat Sheet,  
and Sheet midwifed the perfect shape for my folding's unfolding:  The Rose

I planted it in the communal space that friends and family share;
that sacred garden tilled by our hearts, minds,  and spirits.

Visit it today and throughout the new year,  
and let the forces that raised it  - love,  friendship, courage,  and possibility - salve you.
