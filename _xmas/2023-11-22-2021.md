If 2020 was the year of "what’s?",  perhaps 2021 was the world of 'whens' as we learned to exhale and let Grace weave compassion from chaos.

"What now?" was replaced by "How can I help?" “What am I supposed to do?” was repalced by “Maybe there's something to learn here” and “What does that even…” was replaced by “I guess i don't know either, but I’m here to listen if you need an ear”.

With each replacment we moved one step closer from the head to the heart, where the ‘Whens’ live: 

When we could see the dailyweeklyonthly moments of connection with friends and famiy we've been gifted; When we learned to live the simple spirutal truh that the more you give away, the more capacity you have to *receive* so you can give away again, and when we realized that the need to answer all the 'whys' could be dissolved in moments of gratitude.

If 2020 was about enduring through strange times, 2021 was about seeing what *allowed us to edure* and which will contiue to buoy us forward  through the  new years' tides: family, friends, dogs, cats, laghter, meals, project, faith, compassion, surrender, gratifude, love and forebearnce
